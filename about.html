About Job Finder

Job Finder is an independent job aggregation website focused on remote jobs, online work, and flexible income opportunities.

Our goal is to collect publicly available job listings and present them in a simple, easy-to-browse format for people looking for remote or location-independent work.

Job Finder is not an employer and does not hire candidates directly. We simply provide informational listings and redirect users to official application pages.
